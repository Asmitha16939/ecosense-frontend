// Our Mission

Democratizing Resource Intelligence

EcoSense was founded in 2023 with a simple goal: to help households understand their electricity and water consumption without installing any sensors or smart meters. We've built AI models that can accurately estimate usage based on simple inputs like appliance types, usage hours, and flow intensities.

Our technology has already helped over 10,000 families reduce their utility bills by an average of 30% while contributing to a more sustainable future. We're proud to make resource intelligence accessible to everyone.
